Site Selection & Venue Management

We understand that choosing the perfect destination and venue for your event can be challenging and somewhat nerve-racking. Isn’t it, after all, always about location, location, location?

Along with our great partners and industry relationships, allow Ortiz & Co. to do the research while supporting your ideas and concepts to find the perfect match. Through an initial discovery call, we will be able to understand your event wishes and needs. Our goal is to make the process as smooth as possible so that your team can concentrate on their daily event responsibilities.

In addition to assisting in the search for the perfect venue and destination, our team will also prepare and submit all RFPs. Upon receipt of these proposals, our experts will take the time to review all submissions carefully, so that we’re able to recommend, not only the appropriate locations, but also assist in negotiating the best terms & conditions for your event, at no extra cost to you.

Our support doesn’t end once agreements have been confirmed. Count on the Ortiz & Co. team to continue our collaboration through the event planning development while becoming an extension to your teams.
