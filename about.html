Our team of experienced, service-orientated, and proactive experts will work together with you to create and execute a seamless event within your budget, all without compromising the quality of our customer service. Count on Ortiz & Co. to be a trusted and reliable Event Services company with solutions all blended into one.

With a passion for planning, combined with the pursuit of success for every event, Ortiz & Co. aims to achieve your goals at every event and make the experience one to remember.
